Review gate — the inspector owns the report

The software prepares the paperwork. The inspector owns the report.

Inspector Gadgets does not replace the inspection or the inspector's judgment. It keeps information organized, identifies missing work, and prepares the configured documents. The licensed inspector verifies the property, reviews every finding, confirms the form edition required for the assignment, and signs the final report.

Form requirements and acceptance can vary by carrier, program, and assignment. Confirm the required form and edition before delivery. Inspector Gadgets does not guarantee carrier acceptance or insurance outcomes.
