01 — Research the property

Start with the available property information already pulled in.

Enter the address and review available owner, parcel, year-built, square-footage, flood, roof, permit, and public-source information before the walkthrough. Inspector Gadgets keeps the source links with the job so you can verify what matters.

  • Florida property-record research
  • Permit-history source links
  • Address autocomplete
  • Calendar and ICS import
  • Inspector review before form use

02 — Run the inspection

Follow a guided workflow built around the inspection type.

Choose full-home, 4-point, wind mitigation, roof certification, or a combined inspection. The app shows the systems, fields, and photo requirements that belong to that job so you can see what is complete and what still needs attention.

  • State-approved checklists
  • Required-photo slots
  • Structured system findings
  • Reusable inspector profiles
  • Phone, tablet, and laptop access
Inspection workspaceGuided by type

03 — Organize the photos

Keep the right photos with the right part of the job.

Capture or import multiple photos, place them into labeled slots, and rotate, crop, or brighten them before the report is built. Supported labels and data plates can be scanned for review, but you remain in control of every value used.

  • Labeled photo requirements per inspection type
  • Bulk import that sorts into the right slots
  • Crop, rotate, and brighten in the job
  • On-device label and data-plate scanning
Photo evidenceGuided checklist

04 — Review the work

See what is missing before you leave.

Readiness checks flag incomplete fields, missing required photos, and items that still need inspector review. Inspector Gadgets assists with organization and drafting; the qualified inspector verifies the property, approves the findings, and signs the final report.

  • Readiness view of what still needs attention
  • Approve, edit, or reject every suggestion
  • Unverified fields stay blank, never guessed
  • Signature gate before final export
Review & approveYou have the final say

05 — Deliver the documents

Turn reviewed inspection data into finished PDFs.

Carry approved job information into the configured Florida 4-point and OIR-B1-1802 templates, append organized photo pages, and download the completed documents. Create a client-ready package without retyping the same address, inspector, and system information into separate files.

  • Official Florida 4-Point (2025) and OIR-B1-1802 (Rev. 04/26) PDFs
  • Photo pages appended
  • Print, download, email, or share a view-only link
  • A full Standards-of-Practice report for full-home jobs
