What inspections can I complete with Inspector Gadgets?

Inspector Gadgets supports full-home inspections, Florida 4-point inspections, wind mitigation inspections, roof certification workflows, and combined 4-point plus wind mitigation jobs. The inspection type controls which systems, fields, required photos, and reports appear.

Does it use the official Florida forms?

Yes. Inspector Gadgets prepares the current Florida 4-Point Inspection form and the OIR-B1-1802 Uniform Mitigation Verification Inspection form using the official templates configured in the product. The inspector reviews the information and signs the final report.

Does the software perform the inspection for me?

No. Inspector Gadgets organizes the job, retrieves available records, guides required steps and photos, drafts eligible information, and prepares reports. A qualified inspector performs the inspection, verifies the evidence, approves the findings, and signs the final documents.

What property information can it pull?

Depending on the address and public records available, the app can retrieve owner, parcel, year built, square footage, flood information, permit history, roof details, and source links. Coverage varies by jurisdiction — see the coverage page. Every researched value should be verified before use.

What happens when a record cannot be verified?

The field remains available for inspector review or manual entry. Inspector Gadgets does not invent missing property or permit information.

How does the guided photo system save time?

The required-photo list changes with the inspection type and keeps each photo attached to the correct system or form section. You can see what is still missing before leaving the property, reducing the chance of a return trip.

What does the AI do?

AI-assisted features can analyze supported photos or draft possible findings and form answers for review. OCR can also read supported equipment labels. These features do not finalize reports — the inspector approves, changes, or rejects every suggestion.

Can I complete a full-home inspection report?

Yes. Full-home jobs use structured systems and components based on the Florida Standards of Practice for Home Inspectors. The final report includes conditions, comments, findings, photos, scope, limitations, and inspector information.

Can I send reports from the app?

You can prepare the selected report documents, download or print the PDFs, open a pre-written client email, or create a private document-sharing link when the connected account features are available.

Can I collect the inspection fee?

When client payments are enabled, inspectors can connect their own Stripe account, open a secure client checkout for the inspection fee, and track the payment status with the job. The funds go directly through the inspector's connected Stripe account.

Where is my data stored?

Your inspections sync securely to your Inspector Gadgets account so you can continue on another signed-in device. You can also export and keep a complete copy of each job. See the Trust Center for details.

Am I locked into the software?

No. You can export and keep complete copies of your inspection documents and supporting job information at any time.

Do I need to install anything?

The web application runs in a modern browser on a phone, tablet, or computer. An iOS app may also be available through Apple's distribution channels once released.

Is this only for Florida?

The current production positioning is Florida-first. We do not advertise other states until the relevant checklists, forms, jurisdiction rules, and product QA are complete.
