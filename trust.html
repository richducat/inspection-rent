Built to assist the inspector, not replace one

Your judgment stays in control.

Inspector Gadgets helps with research, organization, photo handling, and paperwork. It does not perform the physical inspection, make the final determination, or sign the report. Those responsibilities stay with the qualified inspector.

Nothing reaches the final report without review

Scanned values, imported records, suggested fields, photos, and generated form entries remain subject to inspector review. Readiness checks identify missing or unresolved work before final export.

Public information comes with its source

Property and permit information depends on the records available for the address and jurisdiction. Inspector Gadgets keeps source links with the job so the inspector can verify the information before using it.

Keep the job available to your account

When account sync is enabled, job information is stored with the Inspector Gadgets account so it can be reopened on supported devices. The app may also keep working data on the device and can support selected Google Drive backup or sharing workflows.

Suggestions are starting points, not findings

Supported scans and photo tools can help organize information or suggest values for review. Availability depends on the configured service and the image quality. The inspector confirms what is correct and decides what belongs in the report.

Confirm the assignment before delivery

The product prepares the form templates configured in the app. The inspector is responsible for confirming the form and edition required by the carrier, program, client, and assignment, then reviewing and signing the final document.

Questions about your data?

Questions about access, deletion, privacy, or security? Review the Privacy Policy or contact support with the email address used for the account.
