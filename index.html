Sec 07 — Fair questions

Asked by inspectors, answered straight.

What does it cost, and what do I have to install?

Nothing to install — it runs in the browser on your phone, tablet, or computer. Your first inspection is the trial: sign up with your email, put a card on file, and run one full job on us. After that it's $5 per individual pull, $20/month for up to 10 inspections, or $98/year for unlimited. Cancel anytime; your saved work stays yours.

Does the AI write my report for me?

It drafts; you decide. The app reads your photos, drafts findings, and pencils in form answers — and every single suggestion waits for you to approve, edit, or reject it. The report can't even be finalized until you've reviewed them all. You are, and stay, the inspector of record.

Will carriers accept these forms?

You export the official Florida 4-Point and the official OIR-B1-1802 wind mitigation, filled in on the real state and carrier templates — not lookalikes. Anything unverified is left blank rather than guessed. Acceptance is always the carrier's call, but your paperwork will never hand an underwriter an easy reason to kick it back.

Where does my clients' data go?

It stays with you. Inspections live on your own device — your subscription pays for the tool, not custody of your files. You can save a complete backup of any inspection whenever you like.

What if I need to change something after I've signed?

Any edit after signing reopens the report, and you sign again. That's on purpose — it means a signed report is always exactly what you approved, nothing more and nothing less.
